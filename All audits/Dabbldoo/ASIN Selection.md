**Prior to Nov 2025**, this product was extremely small: 5-30 sessions/week, 0-3 units/week, averaging roughly $30-60/week in sales from Sep 2024 through Oct 2025.

- **Nov 2025 spike:** Something drove a massive traffic surge. Sessions went from ~10-15/week to 2,242 in the month (roughly 560/week). 152 units sold at ~$17-20 avg price. This was likely an external traffic event (social media, influencer, or viral moment) rather than organic Amazon growth, given the speed of the ramp.
- **Dec 2025 crash:** Buy box collapsed to 48.22%, which killed sales despite the Dec CVR appearing high (13.33%). The high CVR on low sessions is misleading. Sessions dropped 87% from Nov. The buy box loss is the primary driver of the decline.
- **Jan 2026 dead period:** Zero sales, 23 sessions. Likely a stock-out or listing suppression event following the buy box crash.
- **Feb-Mar 2026 recovery:** Buy box restored to 100%. Sessions and sales ramping back up steadily. Mar 2026 ($1,450) is approaching Nov 2025 levels but not there yet.

## Insights

- This is a 100% organic brand with zero ad spend. All current sales are driven by organic traffic and/or external sources. There is significant untapped PPC potential.
- The product went from near-zero to $2,683/month in Nov 2025, suggesting the product has market demand when it gets visibility. The question is what triggered that spike and whether it can be replicated or sustained through PPC.

## Things to Investigate Further

- What caused the Nov 2025 traffic spike? Check SQP data to see if search volume for relevant keywords spiked in Nov 2025, or if this was purely external traffic.
- The buy box crash to 48% in Dec 2025: is this a private label brand with no competing sellers? If so, this points to price changes triggering MAP-related suppression. Need to verify in Product Understanding (Step 2).
- The product was selling at $37.99 early on (single-unit sales in Sep 2024) and now averages $17-21. Was there a price reduction or is the 2-Pack/3-Pack mix shifting the average down?

## Questions for the Seller

- Sales spiked dramatically in Nov 2025 (from ~$120/month to $2,683). Was this driven by a specific external event (TikTok, influencer post, PR)? Understanding this helps determine whether the traffic source is repeatable.
- Buy box dropped to 48% in Dec 2025 and the listing appeared dead in Jan 2026. Was there a stock-out, listing suppression, or pricing change during this period?
- There is zero ad spend across the entire account history. Is this intentional, or has PPC not been explored yet?
