- Revenue cycles hard: summer peak (Jun-Jul ~$13–14K) vs late-fall trough (Nov ~$4.2K). Consistent with the product's sun-exposure use case, but this has not been confirmed with SQP search volume yet.
- Mar 2026 is already back at last summer's peak range, well ahead of where the cycle was in Mar 2025 (no direct comp, but Apr 2025 was only $11.1K). Ad launch in Feb plus seasonality entering spring means Apr-Jun 2026 could exceed last year's summer unless something breaks.
- CVR climbs in low-volume months (to ~70%+) and settles around 55–60% in peak. Reads as branded/repeat traffic dominating off-season, with new-to-brand discovery traffic arriving in peak. The overall CVR ceiling is unusually high either way.

## Insights

- P0 (Lip Balm & SPF 30) is the entire business: 86% of revenue, the only product with ads, and every catalog decision should be centered on it.
- P0 ad economics are healthy from day one: 2.59 ROAS, 2.82% TACoS, 38.3% ad CVR on 360 clicks. Ads are working; the volume is just small relative to the product's potential.
- Ad sales are only 7.3% of P0 revenue, meaning this product is running almost entirely on organic. There is significant room to scale PPC without cannibalizing.

## Things to Investigate Further

- P1 (4 oz. SPF 45) buy box dropped to 80.65% in Feb 2026 before recovering to 94.31% in Mar. Since each parent has exactly one child, this is a true child-level buy box issue, not a parent-aggregation artifact. Possible MAP/price-driven suppression; worth checking price history in Step 2.
- P3 (SPF 45 2 oz.) CVR sits at 26–37% while the rest of the catalog converts at 55–75%. Same brand, similar format (bottle sunscreen) as P1 - investigate listing quality, pricing, or rating gap in Step 2.
- Whether Z Blok's revenue cycle is driven by demand seasonality (search volume) or by ad/stock/listing factors. Verify with SQP 12-month search volume in Step 3 before concluding "seasonal."

## Questions for the Seller

- P1 (4 oz. SPF 45) had a buy box dip to ~81% in Feb. Any price changes, promotions, or MAP-related events around that time?
