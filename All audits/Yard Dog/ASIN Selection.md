- **Strongly seasonal product.** Peak in June-July ($18K-$23K/month), trough in Dec-Feb ($3.5K/month). The current period is the seasonal low. March is the start of the spring ramp, which is the ideal time to invest in growth before the summer peak.

## Insights

- The timing is ideal for this engagement. Mole trap demand ramps March through July. Getting PPC and listing optimization in place now means maximum impact during the peak season.
- The product has strong organic demand ($23K peak month in July 2025). PPC optimization on top of this baseline could meaningfully increase the ceiling.
- Buy box issues are a recurring constraint. The Silver 1 Pack child is broken (33.3% BB, $0 sales). The parent-level buy box dips to 70-85% periodically, which suppresses both organic and ad performance.
- Two dead listings (Single Pack orphan, Plunger+Scissor combo) are wasting catalog space and could be confusing customers who land on them.

## Things to Investigate Further

- What is causing the recurring buy box drops? Is there a competing seller on the listing, or is this a pricing/fulfillment issue? Need to check Keepa data in Step 2.
- The Silver 1 Pack (B08LQWY8V4) has 33.3% buy box and $0 sales. Is this child cannibalizing traffic from the newer Black 1 Pack without converting?
- Is the seasonal pattern purely demand-driven (moles are a spring/summer problem), or is there a stock-out component during winter months?
