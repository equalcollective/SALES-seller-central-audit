- Revenue has declined ~74% from Dec 2024 peak ($1,779/mo) to Mar 2026 ($460/mo). Sessions have declined at a similar rate (~77%), confirming this is a traffic problem, not a conversion problem.
- CVR has actually been stable to slightly improved through the decline (11-17% range), meaning the product converts well when people find it. The issue is visibility.
- Buy box was rock-solid (97-99%) through most of 2025 but has become volatile in Q1 2026 (dipping to 84% in Feb, recovering to 93% in Mar). This is a new development worth investigating.

## Insights

- 100% organic sales with zero ad spend. All traffic is coming from organic search ranking, which has been deteriorating steadily. Without any PPC support, the product has no mechanism to defend or grow its search position.
- The traffic decline is the core problem. CVR is healthy (11-15%), so the product converts when shoppers land on the listing. Growth is entirely blocked by low and declining sessions.

## Things to Investigate Further

- Buy box has dropped from 97-99% (2025 average) to 84-93% in Q1 2026. For a private label brand with no competing sellers, this suggests potential pricing issues or listing suppression. Need to check in Product Understanding.
- Sep 2025 had a notable dip ($560, 214 sessions), then partial recovery in Oct ($960). Need SQP data to determine if this is seasonal search volume or a product-specific event.

## Questions for the Seller

- The brand has never run ads on Amazon. Is this intentional, or has PPC been considered and deprioritized for a reason (budget, margin constraints, lack of expertise)?
