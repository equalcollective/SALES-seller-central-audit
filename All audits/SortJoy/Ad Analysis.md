# Ad Analysis: SortJoy

## No Advertising Data Available

SortJoy is not currently running any Amazon advertising. The Seller Analytics system shows `ads_day_count: 0` with no ad start or end dates.

This is consistent with the SQP findings: SortJoy's Amazon sales are driven almost entirely by branded search (customers who already know the brand from DTC, retail, or social media). Without advertising, the brand has near-zero visibility on non-branded category queries.

## Implications for the Action Plan

The absence of advertising is both the biggest problem and the biggest opportunity:

1. **Problem:** Without PPC, SortJoy cannot be discovered by shoppers searching for "felt storage bins" or related queries. The brand's impression share on Tier 1 queries is just 1.36% (organically), and on Tier 2 it's 0.41%.

2. **Opportunity:** Launching a well-structured PPC campaign on Tier 1 and Tier 2 queries is the single most impactful growth lever available. However, the listing must be improved first (reviews, A+ content, video, bullet optimization) before ad spend will convert efficiently.

3. **Recommended approach:** Fix the listing (Weeks 1-4), then launch PPC (Weeks 4-8). Launching ads on the current listing with zero reviews and no A+ content would result in poor ROAS and wasted spend.

Ad analysis sub-steps (campaign structure, auto vs manual split, profitability, targeting, search term overlap) are not applicable for this seller.
