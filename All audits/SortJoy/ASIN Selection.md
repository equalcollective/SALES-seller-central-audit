# ASIN Selection: SortJoy

## Data Readiness

- **Business data:** Jul 2024 to Mar 15, 2026 (81 weekly periods)
- **Ad data:** None. SortJoy is not running any advertising.
- **Analysis window:** Dec 2025, Jan 2026, Feb 2026 (3 complete months)

## Account Overview

SortJoy sells premium felt storage bins and organizers on Amazon US. The catalog has 25 parent ASINs (68 children), nearly all variations of felt storage bins in different sizes, shapes, and colors (Carbon/Dark Grey and Stone/Oatmeal variants).

**3-Month Account Totals (Dec-Feb):**

| Metric | Dec 2025 | Jan 2026 | Feb 2026 |
|--------|----------|----------|----------|
| Total Sales | $7,954 | $6,761 | $2,666 |
| Units | 128 | 90 | 46 |
| Sessions | 2,255 | 3,329 | 1,847 |
| CVR | 5.68% | 2.70% | 2.49% |
| Buy Box % | 82.5% | 75.6% | 76.2% |

The account is in steep decline: sales dropped 66% from Dec to Feb. Sessions actually increased in Jan before falling, but CVR collapsed from 5.68% to 2.49%. Buy box has been consistently below 80%, which is a structural problem across the catalog.

| Metric | Mar 2025 | Jul 2025 | Dec 2025 | Jan 2026 | Feb 2026 |
|--------|----------|----------|----------|----------|----------|
| Total Sales | $638 | $2,394 | $2,713 | $1,436 | $0 |

| CVR | 2.17% | 8.52% | 4.24% | 1.17% | 0.0% |
| Buy Box % | 95.3% | 97.4% | 98.9% | 92.4% | 85.8% |

- This product grew steadily from $638/mo in Mar to a peak of $2,713 in Dec, then fell off a cliff. In Feb 2026, it generated $0 despite 249 sessions. The collapse is not a traffic problem, it is a conversion problem: CVR went from 4.24% (Dec) to 1.17% (Jan) to 0% (Feb).
- Buy box has been gradually eroding (99% in Dec to 86% in Feb) but is not low enough to fully explain zero sales. Something else changed, possibly pricing, a listing issue, or a stock/fulfillment problem.
- In Sep 2025, buy box dipped to 66.4% and sales dropped proportionally. The current situation is different: buy box is 86% but CVR is zero, suggesting a product page or pricing issue rather than a pure buy box problem.

## Insights

- **Account-wide buy box crisis:** 6 of 25 products have critically low buy box (<60%), and many others hover around 50%. This suggests either unauthorized resellers, pricing disputes, or FBA/FBM competition on SortJoy's own listings. This is the single biggest structural issue in the account.
- **P0 (Sculpted Bins + Lids) has zero sales in Feb despite 249 sessions.** The buy box is still 86%, so something else is blocking conversion. This needs immediate investigation.
- **P1 (Extra Large Sculpted Bins) has a persistent buy box problem** (33-65% since Aug 2025, down from 96-100% before). Sales are directly correlated: when buy box was healthy, the product was doing $2,640/mo. Now it is volatile and underperforming.
- **Felt Large Storage Bins is the only product growing** (Dec $228 to Feb $570), but its buy box is ~47%. If buy box were fixed, this product could scale significantly.

## Things to Investigate Further

- Why did P0's CVR collapse in Jan/Feb despite stable traffic and decent buy box? Check if price changed, listing was altered, or if there is a review issue.
- Why did Clip-On Pantry Labels buy box drop from 99% to 50% in Feb? This is the highest-traffic product (426 sessions) and it went from 15 units in Jan to 6 in Feb.
- Is there a common root cause for the buy box losses across multiple products (resellers, pricing policy, 3P sellers)?

## Questions for the Seller

- Multiple products have buy box below 50%, including several that were at 100% as recently as Aug 2025. Are there unauthorized resellers or other 3P sellers on these listings? Or has there been a change in fulfillment method (FBA to FBM)?
- P0 (Sculpted Bins + Lids) went from $2,713 in Dec to $0 in Feb despite 249 sessions. Has there been a pricing change, stock issue, or listing suppression on this product?
- No advertising is being run on this account. Is this intentional, or has there been a past attempt that was paused?
