## Insights

- **Buy box on P0's top variant is the single biggest constraint on this account.** B07VMJ4PQS generates $1,932 in 3 months with effectively 0% buy box. With the distributor's offer in the buy box, the vast majority of sessions are converting for the distributor, not HLP. Recapturing buy box on this one variant likely doubles P0 revenue immediately.
- **This account runs zero advertising.** All $5,813 in 3-month revenue is 100% organic. This is unusual for a brand with 8 products. It means every sale is earned through organic ranking and brand equity, with no investment in visibility or growth.
- **The catalog is collagen-focused with two non-core outliers.** 6 of 8 products are collagen/protein supplements. Laundry Detergent Sheets and Teeth Whitening Powder appear to be experiments that haven't gained traction.

## Things to Investigate Further

- **Is the buy box loss on B07VMJ4PQS driving customers to buy other variants?** The Vanilla and Unflavored 10 oz variants have growing sales, and some of that growth could be customers who wanted the 1-lb Unflavored but couldn't buy from HLP. If so, solving the buy box issue on the 1-lb could cannibalize some variant sales, but the net effect would still be strongly positive.
- **Why did buy box briefly reach 93% in Aug 2025?** Something changed that month (distributor out of stock? price alignment?). Understanding what happened could inform the strategy for driving the distributor out. This is worth asking the seller about.

## Questions for the Seller

- **What is the distributor's pricing on B07VMJ4PQS (Unflavored, 1 Pound)?** The seller has 1-3% buy box consistently, which suggests the distributor's price is meaningfully lower. Understanding the price gap is critical for the removal strategy.
- **Are there contractual terms with the distributor, or can the relationship be ended immediately?** The timeline for buy box recovery depends on how quickly the distributor's inventory can be depleted or the relationship terminated.
