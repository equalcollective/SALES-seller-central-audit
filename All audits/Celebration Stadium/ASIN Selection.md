- **Buy box collapsed from ~100% to ~75% in September 2025 and has not recovered.** This is the most critical finding. For 6 months, roughly 1 in 4 page views are not showing the seller's offer. This directly suppresses both CVR and sales.
- **Sales have dropped ~63% from peak ($7,131 in Mar to $2,669 in Feb).** Sessions are down even more steeply (4,319 to 1,100, a 75% decline). The product was doing $5-7K/month organically with zero ad spend through most of 2025, but has been declining steadily.
- **CVR has always been low (1.1-3.0%).** Even at peak buy box, this product converts at only 2-3%. For a $90 product on Amazon, this signals friction in the purchase decision, likely related to the product's niche nature (candle holder for birthday cakes) and price point.

## Insights

- **P1 (100 Tall Gold Birthday Candles) is the real revenue engine.** It generates 3x the sales of the candle holder at 5% TACoS with ~100% buy box and 35% CVR. It is overwhelmingly organic. This product is healthy and growing.
- **P0 (Candle Holder) has a buy box problem that must be diagnosed.** The drop from 100% to 75% in September 2025 coincides with the sales decline. This is either a competitor gaining the buy box, a pricing issue, or a fulfillment/seller metric problem.
- **Capital allocation concern:** In January, $1,862 was spent on candle holder ads at 1.43 ROAS (below the 1.5 profitability threshold). Meanwhile, candles generated $11,513 at only $1,060 ad spend with 2.66 ROAS. The candle holder is consuming disproportionate ad budget relative to its returns.

## Things to Investigate Further

- What caused the buy box to drop to 75% in September 2025? Check for competing offers, pricing changes, or fulfillment issues (Step 2).
- Is the candle holder's low CVR (2-3%) inherent to the product category, or is there a listing quality issue driving it? (Step 2e)
- Are the bundle variations (silver, white, mirror) intentionally listed separately or could they be consolidated under the main parent? Several have zero sales and fragmented sessions.

## Questions for the Seller

- The buy box on the Candle Holder dropped from ~100% to ~75% in September 2025 and has stayed there for 6 months. Are you aware of a competitor winning the buy box, or was there a pricing or fulfillment change around that time?
- There are 9+ parent ASINs with zero sales that appear to be bundle variations of the same candle holder. Are these intentionally separate listings, or would consolidating them make sense?
