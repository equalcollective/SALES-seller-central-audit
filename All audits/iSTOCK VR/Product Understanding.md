# Product Understanding: P0 - SMARTstock VR Gunstock (B0FJJNSWB8)

## Product Profile

**Product:**
- A two-handed VR gunstock that physically connects two Meta Quest controllers in a rifle configuration for VR shooting games
- Key differentiators: Ultra-lightweight (~6 oz / 180g, made from PLA Plus), non-magnetic intuitive snap-in connection system (vs. magnetic systems used by competitors), adjustable buttstock with optional cheek rest, compact foldable design
- Solves aiming instability in VR FPS games. Without a gunstock, players hold two separate controllers and coordinate them mid-air to aim. A gunstock locks them into a stable rifle form factor, dramatically improving accuracy and immersion
- Purchase motivation: Competitive advantage and immersion in VR shooters like Contractors, Pavlov, and Onward. The product is trusted by esports athletes in competitive VR leagues (VREL, IVRL, VRML)

**Customer:**
- VR FPS gamers, primarily Meta Quest headset owners (Quest 2, 3, 3S, Pro)
- Range from casual players looking for more immersion to competitive esports players seeking precision
- Male-skewing, tech-savvy, 18-35 demographic
- Purchase driver: frustration with aiming instability using standard controllers, desire for a more realistic shooting experience

**Brand:**
- Registered brand with real identity. Operates under "iSTOCK" branding on Amazon, with a separate sub-brand "SMARTstock" (sold via SKOLVR) and "iSTOCK PRO" as a premium line
- Amazon-native and DTC. Sells through istockgaming.com (Shopify), skolvr.com, Etsy, and Walmart
- Active community presence: Discord server, YouTube channel, affiliate program (10% commission)
- Brand vibe: Tactical/military aesthetic. Listing images use composite battlefield backgrounds, models in military gear. The brand targets the "serious gamer" identity rather than casual/playful

**Competitive Landscape:**
