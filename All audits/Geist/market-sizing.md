### Key Takeaway — Ink and Dye Remover

**Massive impression-to-click gap.** Geist gets thousands of impressions on high-volume ink keywords but almost ZERO clicks. On "ink remover for leather" (1,322 SV), they have 1,131 impressions but only 10 clicks (0.9% CTR vs 1.8% industry). On "ink remover" (2,194 SV), they get 305 impressions and 1 click.

**The problem is NOT visibility — it's the listing/main image/title.** The product is showing up in search results but shoppers are scrolling past it. This is a classic CTR problem that can be fixed with:
1. Better main image (lifestyle shot showing ink being removed from leather)
2. Title optimization to include "leather" and "ink remover" prominently
3. Better price positioning ($37.50 at $5.56/fl oz is premium vs competitors)

**Revenue opportunity if CTR is fixed:**
- "ink remover for leather" alone: 1,300 SV × 2% CTR × 13% CVR × $37 = ~$125/mo just from this one keyword
- Total addressable across all ink/dye keywords: if Geist achieves 2% CTR across the board and 12% CVR → ~$2,500-3,000/mo from organic search alone
- With PPC on top: could realistically reach $4,000-6,000/mo for this single product

**Currently $0 ad spend on this product** — combined with the CTR issue, this is the single biggest low-hanging-fruit opportunity in the Geist catalog.
