# PhysiVantage SQP Keyword Opportunities & Bidding Recommendations

**Date:** March 5, 2026
**Data range:** Q3 2024 – Q4 2025 (SQP quarterly data)

---

## Overall Share of Voice (Collagen Queries)

PhysiVantage's share across collagen-related queries is extremely small:
- **Impression share:** 0.02–0.03%
- **Click share:** 0.02–0.03%
- **Purchase share:** 0.02–0.03%

This is expected for a niche brand competing against Vital Proteins (80K+ reviews), Ancient Nutrition (47K+), and Sports Research (15K+). The strategy should NOT be to compete on broad terms but to **dominate niche, high-intent queries** where PhysiVantage has a right to win.

---

## TIER 1: MUST-BID KEYWORDS (High Priority)

These are keywords where PhysiVantage has proven conversion data, strong relevance, and/or a unique right to win.

### 1. "collagen" (head term)
- **Volume:** ~1.3M–1.5M/quarter
- **Brand performance:** 37–61 clicks/quarter, 8–17 purchases/quarter consistently
- **Why bid:** This is the #1 volume driver. Even at 0.01% purchase share, PhysiVantage gets 8–17 sales/quarter from this single keyword. With low impression share (0.01%), there's massive room to scale impressions. The absolute purchase numbers justify spend.
- **Bid strategy:** Moderate bid, product targeting. Don't try to win top of search — too expensive. Focus on rest-of-search and product page placements where the price-per-click is manageable.

### 2. "collagen powder"
- **Volume:** ~350K–540K/quarter
- **Brand performance:** 13–23 clicks/quarter, 3–6 purchases/quarter
- **Why bid:** Second-highest converter. More specific than "collagen" so slightly better qualified traffic. PhysiVantage has 0.02% impression share — room to grow.
- **Bid strategy:** Moderate bid, both Sponsored Products and Sponsored Brands.

### 3. "collagen peptides"
- **Volume:** ~1.4M–1.8M/quarter
- **Brand performance:** 17–31 clicks/quarter, 1–5 purchases/quarter
- **Why bid:** Huge volume, proven conversions. Lower CVR than "collagen" but the sheer volume makes it worthwhile.
- **Bid strategy:** Conservative bid. This is expensive and competitive. Run exact match at low bids to cherry-pick profitable placements.

### 4. "collagen for athletes"
- **Volume:** ~290–544/quarter (small but growing)
- **Brand performance:** 1–16 clicks/quarter, 0–2 purchases/quarter
- **Impression share:** 2–4% (10x higher than head terms)
- **Why bid:** THIS IS PHYSIVANTAGE'S HOME TURF. 3.75% impression share means they're already showing up. The category converts well (industry CVR 7–13%). PhysiVantage has the most relevant product for this query. Cart adds are strong (3–5/quarter). The lack of consistent purchases despite cart adds suggests a pricing/listing conversion issue, not a relevance problem.
- **Bid strategy:** Aggressive bid. Top of search. This is a must-own keyword. Consider Sponsored Brand video ad for differentiation.

### 5. "collagen for tendon repair" / "collagen tendon"
- **Volume:** ~1,000/quarter
- **Brand performance:** 10 clicks, 2 cart adds, 1 purchase in Q3 2024 (best quarter)
- **Impression share:** 4.5% — highest among collagen queries
- **Why bid:** Extremely high intent. People searching this are looking for exactly what PhysiVantage offers. The product is literally designed for tendon support with Vitamin C co-factor. PhysiVantage has ~4.5% impression share here — credible.
- **Bid strategy:** Aggressive bid, exact match. Sponsored Brand headline: "Supercharged Collagen — Formulated for Tendon & Ligament Recovery"

### 6. "collagen joint supplement" / "collagen joint support"
- **Volume:** ~750–1,460/quarter
- **Brand performance:** 1 click, 1 cart add, 1 purchase on "collagen joint supplement" in Q4 2025
- **Why bid:** High-intent joint health queries. PhysiVantage is relevant (joint support is a core claim). The industry CVR on these queries is 7–11% — people who search this are ready to buy.
- **Bid strategy:** Moderate-aggressive bid. Exact and phrase match.

## TIER 2: HIGH-OPPORTUNITY KEYWORDS (Should Bid)

These are keywords where PhysiVantage shows strong impressions but needs to convert better.

### 7. "tendon and ligament supplements" / "ligament and tendon supplements"
- **Volume:** 4,000–8,000/quarter (significant!)
- **Brand impressions:** 1,000–2,900/quarter (strong presence)
- **Brand clicks:** Only 2–23/quarter despite massive impressions
- **Brand purchases:** 0–1 (almost zero)
- **Why bid:** PhysiVantage gets 1–2.5% impression share organically on this query — that's 10x better than head terms. But the CTR is terrible (0.1–0.8% vs industry 1.5–2.2%). This suggests the listing title/main image isn't compelling enough when shown against pill-based tendon supplements. The opportunity is to use a Sponsored Brand ad with custom creative that says "Collagen-Based Tendon & Ligament Support — With Vitamin C & BCAAs" to differentiate from glucosamine/chondroitin pill products.
- **Bid strategy:** Moderate bid with Sponsored Brand ad (not just Sponsored Products). The creative needs to make clear this is a collagen-based approach to tendon health.

### 8. "tendon repair supplement" / "tendon supplement"
- **Volume:** 1,000–2,800/quarter
- **Brand impressions:** 150–500/quarter
- **Brand clicks:** 0–5/quarter
- **Why bid:** Same logic as above. High relevance, weak CTR. PhysiVantage's product is literally for tendon repair but the listing isn't winning clicks against pill-format competitors. This needs creative differentiation in ads.
- **Bid strategy:** Moderate bid, Sponsored Brands with custom headline.

### 9. "bcaa and collagen powder" / "bcaa collagen" / "collagen bcaa"
- **Volume:** 142–249/quarter (small but PERFECT fit)
- **Brand impressions:** 51–202/quarter (3–4% share)
- **Brand clicks:** 1–8/quarter
- **Brand purchases:** 0–1
- **Why bid:** This is the EXACT product description. Anyone searching "bcaa collagen" is looking for PhysiVantage's product or something very similar. There are almost no competitors here. PhysiVantage should OWN this search. The volume is small but the intent is perfect. "collagen bcaa" drove 1 purchase in Q3 2025 — 100% of all purchases on that query.
- **Bid strategy:** Aggressive bid, exact match. Own top of search. This is a "no-brainer" keyword with near-zero competition and perfect product-market fit.

### 10. "pre workout no caffeine" / "caffeine free pre workout"
- **Volume:** 4,000–21,000/quarter
- **Brand purchases:** 2–4/quarter on "pre workout no caffeine"
- **Why bid:** PhysiVantage CRUSH (pre-workout) appears to be driving these. If they're selling their pre-workout product, this is a proven converter. 1.59% purchase share on "pre workout no caffeine" is excellent for a niche brand.
- **Bid strategy:** Continue bidding. This is a separate product line (CRUSH) but validates the cross-product opportunity.

### 11. "collagen for men" / "collagen for athletes men"

- **Why bid:** PhysiVantage's athletic positioning appeals more to men than beauty-positioned competitors. The "collagen for men" query has decent volume and PhysiVantage could differentiate with its sports/performance messaging. Currently getting impressions but zero conversions — needs listing optimization first (consider making a male-athlete-focused A+ content variation).

### 12. "collagen peptides powder"

- **Bid strategy:** Moderate bid with Sponsored Brand highlighting the complete protein angle.
