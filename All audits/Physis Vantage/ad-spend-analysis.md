**Date:** March 5, 2026

**Total search terms:** 549 | **Total spend:** ~$2,350 | **Total ad sales:** ~$7,900

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

PhysiVantage's ad spend is heavily concentrated on branded and product-name terms, which perform extremely well (13–35x ROAS). Generic collagen head terms are being tested but with mixed results. There is significant wasted spend on irrelevant terms (pre-workout, type-2 collagen, men's collagen) that need immediate negative keyword action. The most exciting finding is a cluster of high-intent long-tail terms (tendon, climbing, athlete, joint pain) converting at 100% CVR with minimal spend — these need aggressive scaling.
