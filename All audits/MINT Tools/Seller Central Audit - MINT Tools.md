## Section 2: Qualitative Product Understanding (P0)

**Product:**
- Professional extra-long-barrel ceramic curling iron, 1/1.25/1.5 inch barrel sizes (3 child ASINs). Listed since May 2019 (7 years).
- Key features: 2-heater ceramic ionic tourmaline barrel, extra-long barrel (no hair overlap), EasyGlide clamp, 270-430°F adjustable, 60-min auto shut-off, international dual voltage 120-240V
- Value prop: Salon-grade tool that works faster than standard irons because of the extended barrel and dual heater design. "Used in 25,000+ professional salons" is the brand's primary trust anchor
- Purchase motivation: Customers stepping up from drugstore irons (Conair, Revlon $30-60) who don't want to pay T3 / GHD / Bio Ionic prices ($130-200+)

**Customer:**
- Adult women, 25-55, with medium-to-long hair who style at home regularly. Travel-conscious shoppers value the dual voltage. Long-hair customers specifically benefit from the extra-long barrel.
- Purchase driver: Frustration with cheap irons that take too long, leave overlap marks, or burn hair. Looking for a "real" tool without paying premium prices.

**Brand:**
- Registered private-label hair tools brand. Active brand store. No competing 3P sellers on listings.
- Established Amazon-native brand with 21 parent ASINs across irons, wands, dryers, flat irons, brushes, wavers, crimpers
- Mid-premium, salon-grade positioning consistent across catalog
- The Mermaid Waver (P1) has built strong branded recognition: "mint s waver" gets 1,800+ monthly searches with MINT taking 86% of purchases. Compare to the curling iron at 340 monthly branded searches - the waver is the brand's stronger consumer story.

## Section 6: Action Plan

The primary blocker is CVR on the hero ASIN, anchored by the 75% buy box. Eight months of suppression. Until this is fixed, every PPC dollar pushed at Tier 1 keywords burns at sub-2x ROAS. Sequence: fix buy box first, fix the ad mis-allocations in parallel (they don't depend on each other), then scale impressions once CVR recovers.

### Weeks 1-2: Immediate Actions (PPC + Pricing)

The blocker is buy box + ad mis-allocation. Both can move in week one.

- **Drop curling iron parent (B07RM2VQ77) price back to ~$89.** This is the price level at which buy box was 99% before the August 2025 increase to $109. Test for 1-2 weeks and monitor whether Amazon restores buy box. Confirm with seller first whether the price increase was intentional and what the constraint was.
- **Pause the 4 zero-sale ad campaigns** (CI125-EX, CW1-EX, BDB-PH-4, BDB-EX-2). Reduce bids on RCI exact-match campaigns. ~$1,200 of $2,000 wasted spend stops bleeding immediately.
- **Add negative keywords across all P0 campaigns:** "beach waver", "curling iron" (in exact only), "1 inch curling wand", "1 inch curling wand long barrel". ~$979 savings over 90 days.
- **Increase Top of Search bid modifier to 75-100% on top P0 campaigns** (CI15-AUTO-ALL, CI125-AUTO-ALL, CI15-BR-AL, CI125-PH-Branded). Reduce Product Pages bid modifier toward zero on the same campaigns. This is the single largest PPC lever in the audit - $48K annualized impact.

### Weeks 2-4: Short-Term Optimizations
