## ⚠️ Critical flag on P0: Buy box at 74% on a private label brand

**The curling iron parent has been sitting at 74-75% buy box since August 2025. Eight straight months. There are no competing 3P sellers on this listing (verified via Keepa offer history: zero offers).**

For a private label brand without 3P competition, the only buy box loss reason is MAP-related suppression by Amazon's algorithm, typically triggered by sudden price changes. The price history confirms it: in Jul 2025 the SKUs sold at $79-89 (Prime Day promos); by Sep 25 2025 the SKUs jumped to $109.97. That ~37% upward jump from a deep promo to a higher MSRP is exactly the pattern that triggers Amazon's pricing algorithm to withhold buy box.

Buy box dropped from 99% (Jul 2025) to 75% (Aug 2025) and never recovered.

This is the single largest immediate growth lever in the audit. **At $480K trailing annual revenue on this product, recovering buy box from 75% to 99% would unlock roughly $150-160K in additional annual sales** (the ~25% buy box gap directly converts to lost sessions converting elsewhere).

## Why Q1 sales are below recent trend

Q1 2026 P0 sales of $98K = ~$33K/month. April 2025-Aug 2025 averaged $35-40K/month BEFORE the buy box drop. So Q1 2026 is roughly in line with the post-drop new normal. The "recovering" trend label refers to month-over-month growth within Q1 (Jan $24K → Feb $34K → Mar $39K), driven by ad spend ramp from $744 in Jan (partial) to $8.5K in Mar. Without buy box recovery, the ceiling on this product is bounded below historical levels.

## Insights

- **P0 buy box at 74% with no 3P competition is the single biggest unlock in this account.** Eight months of suppression suggests the seller hasn't connected the August 2025 price increase (from $89 to $109.97) to the buy box drop. Fixing this is the fastest path to revenue.
- **Account-level buy box dropped from 99% (Jun 2025) to 87-89% (Aug 2025+).** This is mostly P0's contribution diluting the account average, since P0 is the largest revenue driver. Other products mostly retain 99%+ buy box.
- **The 2026 REVAMP curling iron (B0GGJ7P2VJ) is launching alongside the original.** Same SKU lineup, $20 higher price point, 4-heater vs 2-heater. With B07RM2VQ77's buy box broken and the REVAMP at 100% buy box, there may be an unspoken strategy to phase out the old SKU - but Q1 2026 data shows the original outsold the REVAMP 33-to-1, so any phase-out is far from complete.

## Things to Investigate

- **P0 (Curling Irons) buy box mechanics:** Confirm via Seller Central listing page whether the price-history data accurately reflects the listed retail price during the August 2025 buy box drop. If the Keepa-captured prices are correct, the path is clear: drop the price to ~$89 (matching the pre-drop level) and monitor whether Amazon restores buy box.
- **2026 REVAMP rollout strategy:** Is the seller planning to sunset B07RM2VQ77 in favor of B0GGJ7P2VJ? If yes, the REVAMP is currently underspending on ads. If no, the two SKUs will continue to cannibalize each other and the brand should consolidate them under a single parent ASIN.
