## P0 Annual Trend

Brand launched Dec 2024 with a viral TikTok-driven peak. Performance has not recovered to that level since.

- **The brand had a viral launch in Dec 2024, then never recovered.** Dec 2024 = $6,587. Dec 2025 = $2,890 (down 56% YoY despite holiday tailwind). The TikTok-driven traffic that powered the launch has decayed and was not replaced by paid demand generation.
- **Strong Q4 / Christmas seasonality** (per seller: this is a holiday gift product). Dec is the peak; off-season months (May-Oct) bump along $500-$1,200/month. The Apr 2025 secondary peak likely reflects Easter or family-gathering season.
- **Ads turned on ~Jan 26, 2026 and have been unprofitable from day one.** ROAS averaged 0.65 across the 3-month ad window vs. an estimated break-even ROAS of ~2.0 (retail $19.95, COGS $4-5, Amazon fees ~$6.50). Ads are accelerating cash burn, not recovering momentum.
- **CVR has degraded.** Dec 2024 CVR was 2.41% on 11K sessions. Mar 2026 CVR is 1.72% on 2.4K sessions. Even off-peak, CVR was 2-3% under Sophie Society. The current 1% range (some weeks 0.8%) is well below the brand's own historical norm.
- **Buy box is healthy** (99-100%) and is not a constraint anywhere in the timeline.

## Insights

- **The growth path is recovering organic CVR plus profitable ad scaling, not topline ad volume.** Sessions are coming in (200-800/week even in the off-season) but converting at 1% vs. the brand's historical 2-3%. Pushing more paid traffic on top of a 1% CVR is what's driving the negative ROAS.
- **Avg price was reduced from $19.95 to ~$9-10 for the first half of March 2026** (week of Mar 8 weighted avg $9.06). That coincides with the worst CVR weeks. Either the discount didn't move the needle or it cannibalized the listing's perceived value, possibly both.

## Things to Investigate Further

- Was Sophie Society running ads during Dec 2024 / Dec 2025? The 89-day ad window can't see those periods, so we cannot tell if the historical peaks were organic only or ad-supported. (Will be addressed in Step 4 by asking the seller; the data tools cannot answer this.)
- Why is CVR at 1% now vs. 2-3% historically? Listing changes, review degradation, or competitive shift? (Step 2 product understanding will look at rating trajectory and sales rank history; Step 3 SQP will reveal whether the queries the brand ranks for have shifted.)

## Questions for the Seller

- The brand had a clear viral launch in Dec 2024 ($6,587 in one month). What drove it - a specific TikTok creator, a coordinated influencer push, or organic virality? Knowing the trigger informs whether it's repeatable.
- Dec 2025 was 56% below Dec 2024 despite the holiday tailwind. Hypothesis: the TikTok virality faded and was not replaced. Did anything change on the listing, packaging, or marketing side between the two holidays?
- Ads launched in late Jan 2026 at unprofitable ROAS. Was this a self-managed launch or did Sophie Society set up the campaigns before disengaging? Knowing campaign provenance affects what we recommend tearing down vs. optimizing.
