## Tagging Rationale

- **Tier 1 (Hero - exact intent, eyelet reinforcement):** Queries where the customer is specifically searching for a solution to reinforce or fix tarp grommets/eyelets. The product is a direct answer.
  - Keywords: `grommets for tarps`, `tarp grommet kit`, `tarp grommet fasteners`, `tarp grommets`, `tarp fasteners for grommets`

- **Tier 2 (Core market - tarp repair tape/patches):** Queries for tarp repair tape, patches, and kits. P0 is one of several solutions (duct-tape-style repair tape, brass grommet kits, patch kits). Broader competitive set, same "I need to fix my tarp" customer.
  - Keywords: `tarp repair kit`, `tarp repair kit heavy duty`, `tarp repair tape`, `tarp patch kit`, `tarp tape`, `tarp tape heavy duty waterproof`

- **Tier 3 (Broad/adjacent):** Generic tarp accessories discovery searches.
  - Keywords: `tarp accessories`

Queries excluded on purpose:
- `tarp` (300-470k volume) and `tarp clips*` are too broad or wrong product intent (tarp clips are a separate fastening product). Hippo gets effectively zero share there; not a capturable growth lever for P0.

**Notes on the blocker table:**

- **Tier 1 (grommet queries):** Brand has been holding ~1.8% share all year, well below the adjusted ~16-18% cap that two products ranking makes possible. CTR is on par with industry when the brand appears on the search page, which says the listing (image, title, rating) is not the issue for clicks; the listing just is not showing up enough. The ATC rate (12% vs industry 28%) and CVR (3.7% vs 13%) gaps are real but the volume is still too thin to commit to. CVR could also improve as traffic quality increases through more targeted keywords.

- **Tier 2 (tarp repair tape/kit queries):** This is the more urgent story. The brand's Tier 2 impressions dropped from ~2,000 in Apr-Jul 2025 to ~500-800 by Jan-Mar 2026. Over the same window, P0 parent revenue fell from ~$2,900/mo peak to ~$1,200/mo. The loss of Tier 2 visibility tracks the revenue decline almost 1:1. The most likely driver is a pullback in paid impressions on these queries (ad spend or bids reduced mid-year), since ad data only goes back to Jan 2026 and can't be confirmed directly. This is the primary thing to diagnose in Step 4.

- **Branded queries:** Branded queries are absent from the top 50 tagged list (no "hippo" queries with meaningful volume). The brand does not have meaningful branded search - not unusual at this stage, and means there is no branded defense campaign required yet.

## Insights

- P0 (Grommet Reinforcement Tape): Tier 2 (tarp repair tape/kit) impression share collapsed from ~1.8% (Apr 2025) to ~0.7% (Mar 2026). Brand impressions on these queries are down 60% YoY. This mirrors the 60% revenue decline on the P0 parent and appears to be the primary driver, not seasonality. Fixing visibility on Tier 2 is the single biggest lever for recovering lost revenue.
- P0 (Grommet Reinforcement Tape): The brand's CTR on Tier 1 queries (1.91%) is slightly above industry (1.81%). The listing image, title, rating, and price combination is clicking at market rate when it does show up. That is important because it means the scaling problem is visibility, not presentation.
- P0 (Grommet Reinforcement Tape): Market is not seasonal in the way the seller's sales pattern suggests. Tier 1 search volume peaks in Oct-Nov (pre-winter tarp prep) and Tier 2 peaks in Aug-Oct. The seller's revenue peaked May-Jul and declined through the market's actual peak season, so the "sales are seasonal" assumption is wrong - this is a brand-specific decline.

## Things to Investigate Further (Step 4 Ad Analysis)

- Confirm in Step 4 whether Tier 2 paid impression share was reduced (ad spend pulled, bids lowered, campaigns paused) during the second half of 2025. This is the most likely explanation for the Tier 2 impression collapse.
- Check in Step 4 whether the P0 hero child (B09TB3FPKW) is receiving the bulk of ad spend across Tier 1 and Tier 2 queries, or whether spend is scattered across lower-performing children.
- Tier 1 CVR is below industry but base is very thin. After scaling traffic, re-check CVR in 30-60 days to see whether the gap remains real or was a volume artifact.
