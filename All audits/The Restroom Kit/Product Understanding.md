# Product Understanding - The Restroom Kit

## Product Profile: P0 - Restroom Kit (12-Pack)

**Child ASIN used for analysis:** B079LX5T6R (Blue, 12 Count) | Keepa ID: 3722

### Product

- A portable, individually sealed, all-in-one restroom hygiene kit containing a patented oversized toilet seat cover, 3 feet of 3-ply toilet paper, a flushable tush wipe, and a hand wipe
- Key differentiator: the seat cover is patented, oversized for full coverage with a rear pocket for stability. Competitors typically offer standard-size covers that shift or tear. The all-in-one bundled format (cover + paper + wipes) is also distinctive vs. buying components separately
- Solves the problem of using dirty, under-supplied public restrooms. One sealed packet gives you everything you need
- Purchase motivation: peace of mind and hygiene when using public or portable toilets, especially for travel, camping, concerts/festivals, school-age children, and elderly care

### Customer

- **Primary:** Parents (especially mothers) with school-age children who use public restrooms at school, sports, and travel. Women who want hygiene assurance in public restrooms
- **Secondary:** Travelers (road trips, airports, hotels), outdoor enthusiasts (camping, hiking, festivals), caregivers for elderly
- **Purchase driver:** Germaphobia / hygiene anxiety around public restrooms. The emotional trigger is protection and preparedness

### Brand

- **The Restroom Kit** is a registered brand, manufactured by TimeAway LLC
- Website exists (www.TheRestroomKit.com, shown on packaging), but the brand appears to be Amazon-primary with limited DTC or retail distribution
- Brand maturity: Established, niche. Has been selling on Amazon since at least 2018 (B01M1BCJTC ASIN dates back years). Multiple pack sizes and a women's variant indicate product line development over time
- The product is patented (US Patent 7,774,869 B2 and US D451,178 S, visible on packaging)
- **Brand vibe:** Clean, approachable, slightly humorous ("Protect Your Bottom" shield logo). Blue and orange color scheme suggests trust and energy. Not clinical, not luxury. Practical and family-friendly

### Competitive Landscape

- **Price positioning:** Avg comparable 12-pack: ~$25-30 | P0: ~$28 | Positioned mid-market
