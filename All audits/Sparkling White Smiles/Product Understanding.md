# Product Understanding: P0 - Sport Mouth Guards (2 Pack)

**ASIN:** B01A1GF82C | **Keepa ID:** 101985

## Product Profile

**Product:**
- Clear boil-and-bite sport mouth guard, sold as a 2-pack with a free vented carrying case
- Key features: BPA-free, latex-free, no color additives, thick cushioning base for shock absorption, custom-fit to any mouth size
- Value prop: affordable teeth and jaw protection for athletes across all contact sports
- Purchase motivation: parents equipping young athletes for sports seasons, or adult athletes in combat/contact sports needing a reliable, budget-friendly option. The 2-pack format is convenient (backup or sibling use)

**Customer:**
- Target buyer: parents of youth athletes (football, basketball, lacrosse, hockey) and adult athletes in combat sports (boxing, MMA, wrestling, jiu-jitsu)
- Purchase driver: need for mandatory or recommended mouth protection at an accessible price point, with the safety assurance of BPA/latex-free materials

## Brand

- **Sparkling White Smiles** is a registered dental lab brand with 20+ years of experience, primarily focused on custom teeth whitening trays and dental night guards
- The brand has a DTC website (sparklingwhitesmiles.com) selling custom night guards ($60-180 range) and whitening products
- Sport mouth guards are a secondary product line. The brand's core identity is dental lab/whitening, not athletic gear
- Brand vibe: clinical and functional, not sporty or athletic. The website and packaging lean into dental expertise rather than sports performance. This creates a mismatch with the sport mouth guard customer who expects brand signals around athletic performance, durability, and sport-specific design
