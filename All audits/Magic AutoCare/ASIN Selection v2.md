## Insights

- **Capital misallocation across the catalog.** P0 generates 59% of total sales but receives only 34% of ad spend ($6,025 of $17,816). Meanwhile, P1 + P2 + P3 combined consume 53% of ad spend ($9,441) while generating only 33% of sales. P0's ROAS (1.83) far exceeds P1 (1.10), P2 (0.82), and P3 (0.79). Budget rebalancing toward P0 is the most obvious efficiency lever.

- **P0 ad dependency is increasing.** Ad sales %: 20.9% (Dec) to 34.5% (Jan) to 44.5% (Feb). Ad spend ramping: $931 to $2,077 to $3,017. Organic base is steady at $5-7K/month, but all recent growth is ad-driven. TACoS still reasonable at 22.5% but trending up.

## Things to Investigate Further

- **P3 (Ceramic Coating PRO 10H) sharp decline in Feb.** Sales dropped 70% ($1,480 to $440), CVR fell from 4.53% to 1.69%, and buy box dropped to 90.74%. Something specific happened. Need to check via Keepa and SQP data whether this is a traffic, conversion, or competitive issue.

- **P1 (Diamond AI Coating) structurally low CVR** (~1.4% across all three months vs. P0's 5.6%). At $4,476 in 3-month ad spend with only 1.10 ROAS, need to understand if this is a pricing issue, listing issue, or targeting mismatch.

- **P0 buy box crash in Oct 2025** (66.27%). Need to check Keepa for new 3P sellers or pricing events. Important because it could recur.

- **P3 (Ceramic Coating PRO 10H):** Sales dropped 70% in Feb. Stock-out, intentional pause, or something external? Hypothesis: possible listing suppression or competitor price-cutting.
