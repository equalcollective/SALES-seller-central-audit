## P0 Annual Trend: Balloon Shine Spray (B0D4RZYQ7F)

| Metric | Mar 2025 | Jun 2025 (Peak) | Oct 2025 | Feb 2026 |
|--------|----------|----------------|----------|----------|
| Total Sales | $8,454 | $23,066 | $7,520 | $2,858 |
| Sessions | 4,717 | 6,299 | 2,690 | 603 |
| CVR | 10.3% | 22.8% | 17.1% | 28.7% |
| Buy Box % | 53.0% | 66.6% | 63.1% | 66.4% |

- P0 is in a significant downtrend. Sales peaked at $23K in Jun 2025 and have declined 88% to $2.9K by Feb 2026. Sessions dropped from 6,299 to 603 over the same period. This looks seasonal: balloon/party demand peaks in spring-summer (graduation, weddings, outdoor events) and troughs in winter.
- CVR has actually improved as sessions dropped, reaching 28.7% in Feb 2026. This suggests the product converts well among the customers who do find it. The issue is traffic, not conversion. Buy box has stabilized in the 66-72% range after a bad dip to 25-35% in Jul-Sep 2025.
- The Jul-Sep buy box collapse (from 67% to 25%) coincided with the sharpest sales decline. Buy box has recovered, but sessions and sales have not recovered proportionally, pointing to a seasonal demand floor.

## Insights

- **Helium Tank is the real revenue driver but has a broken buy box.** It generated $35.5K in 3 months with 0% ad spend, but buy box sits at 6-13%. With $27.4K in Feb alone (Valentine's spike), fixing buy box could unlock massive revenue. This is the single biggest revenue opportunity in the account.
- **Life Size Cardboard Cutout is heavily ad-dependent and unprofitable.** TACoS of 51-67% with ROAS barely above 1.0x means every dollar of ad spend generates roughly $1 in sales, well below break-even. When ads stopped in Feb, sales collapsed to $376 (from $2.4K-$3.6K). This product has almost no organic demand.
- **Big Head Cardboard Cutout is a money pit.** $1,468 in ad spend generated only $446 in ad sales (0.30 ROAS). TACoS exceeded 200%. This should be paused immediately.

## Things to Investigate Further

- P0 (Balloon Shine Spray) seasonal pattern needs confirmation via SQP data. If the market itself contracts in winter, the decline is expected and the play is to maximize capture during the high season.
- P1 (Helium Tank) buy box at 6-13% on a $35K product, need to understand who else has the buy box and whether this is fixable.

## Questions for the Seller

- Helium Tank generated $27.4K in Feb alone at 13% buy box. Who else is selling on this listing, and is there a strategy to recover buy box? At even 50% buy box, this could be a $100K+/mo product.
- Ads stopped running entirely from Feb 1 onward. Was this intentional, or did something break? The Balloon Shine Spray campaigns were the only ones with any ad spend in Dec-Jan.
- Are the Cardboard Cutout products (Life Size and Big Head) strategic, or would you consider pausing ad spend on them? Combined they spent $5,127 in Dec-Jan with under 1.0x ROAS.
