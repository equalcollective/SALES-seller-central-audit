# Product Understanding: P0 - Balloon Shine Spray (DecoShine)

**ASIN:** B0D4S92RYK (Clear Shineon variant) | **Parent:** B0D4RZYQ7F | **Keepa ID:** 140331

## Product Profile

**Product:**
- Aerosol balloon shine spray (20.28 oz / 600ml) that gives latex balloons a high-gloss, reflective finish and slows oxidation
- Largest single-can size on the market. No-wipe aerosol formula, dries naturally without rubbing, gloves, or cloths. Unscented
- Solves the problem of dull, oxidized balloons at events. Keeps arches, garlands, and balloon displays photo-ready for longer
- Professional balloon decorators buy it as an essential tool for event work. DIY party planners buy it for that "wow factor" on Instagram-worthy balloon displays

**Customer:**
- Primary: Professional balloon decorators and event planners who need reliable, fast-application shine for large installations
- Secondary: DIY party planners (weddings, birthdays, baby showers) who want salon-quality balloon displays at home
- The purchase is driven by visual quality. Oxidized balloons look cheap. Shine spray is the difference between amateur and professional results

## Brand

- **DECOCHAMP** is the consumer brand, **FESTI USA** is the wholesale distribution entity based in Houston, TX
- Registered brand with real identity, not white-label. Started as a family-owned business creating unique balloon decorations (their signature product is the "Starburst" balloon)
- **Wholesale-first brand expanding to Amazon.** Wide distribution through professional balloon supply retailers including Bargain Balloons USA, Premium Party Distribution, City Balloons Dallas, and others
- Full product ecosystem under the "Deco" naming convention: DecoShine (shine spray), DecoPop (balloon popper), DecoFloat (treatment fluid), DecoPump, DecoRuler, DecoBalls
- **Brand vibe:** Professional and functional, targeting the balloon decorator trade. Clean packaging with playful party-themed branding. Not premium-luxury, not budget either. Positioned as the professional's choice
