## Insights

- **P0 (Tablets) is carrying the brand.** It generates 81% of account revenue and absorbs 82% of ad spend. Any meaningful growth for SnoreStop runs through this ASIN.
- **P0 (Tablets) has very high CVR (32%).** This is exceptionally strong and suggests the listing is converting well when it gets traffic. The blocker is almost certainly traffic/visibility, not conversion.
- **P1 (Spray) ads are burning money.** $143 spent to generate $103 in sales (ROAS 0.72, ACoS 138%). This should be paused or restructured immediately.
- **The brand is small.** ~$2K/month in total account revenue. All growth recommendations need to be sized accordingly. Even 3x growth is only ~$6K/month, and the fastest path there is expanding P0's visibility.

## Things to Investigate

- **P0 (Tablets) session decline:** Sessions dropped from ~780/mo (Q1 2025) to ~370/mo (Q1 2026), a ~53% decline. Investigate in Step 3 (SQP) whether search volume for pet snoring queries is declining, whether the brand is losing rank on core keywords, or whether this is a function of the price increase.
- **Ads data gap:** We only have data from 2026-01-18 onward. Confirm in Step 4 whether the seller ran ads before this window. If yes, we should ask them for historical context.
- **P0 (Tablets) traffic recovery in 2026:** Sessions stabilized around 370/mo in Q1 2026 after the long decline. Is this a floor, or is there a seasonal effect we should check in SQP?

## Questions for the Seller

- **Price change:** You moved P0 (Tablets) from ~$10 to ~$15 between April and June 2025. What drove that decision, and did you expect the traffic decline?
- **Buy box dips:** P0 (Tablets) saw buy box drop to 74.7% in May 2025, 72.4% in Nov 2025, and 83.1% in Dec 2025. Since this appears to be a private label listing, the most likely cause is a sudden price change triggering MAP-related suppression. Were there promos or price tests in those months?
- **Duplicate listings:** B0D4JK6VMW and B0D4JKP7SH look like duplicate listings of your active spray and tablets ASINs but have essentially no traffic. What were they created for, and do they still need to exist?
- **P1 (Spray) strategy:** Are you actively trying to grow the spray product, or is it secondary to the tablets? Ads are running unprofitably.
