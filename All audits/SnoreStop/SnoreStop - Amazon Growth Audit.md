## The Growth Path to $5,000/month

Three levers stacked on top of each other. Each is independently meaningful; together they compound.

### Lever 1: PPC Efficiency on the Tablets (immediate, 30 days)

Today, **96% of your ad budget runs through a single broad-match campaign**, with most of the spend going to one keyword (`+snore`) at a 1.86 ROAS. Inside that same campaign, your **best-performing exact-match keywords are running at 7.5x ROAS but starved of budget**.

What we'll do:
- Restructure into focused exact-match campaigns on proven winners (`stop dog snoring`, `anti snoring for dogs`, `snore stop dogs`, etc.).
- Negate human-snoring search terms (the brand is human + pet, but the listing is pet-only, so human-intent clicks are wasted).
- Shift placement bids toward Top of Search (which converts at 2.6x ROAS) and away from Rest of Search (0.23 ROAS, currently leaking ~$180/quarter).

**Expected impact:** ~$300-500/month in additional revenue from the same ad budget, recovered from existing waste.

### Lever 2: Activate the Spray to Capture Tier 2 (30-60 days)

Tier 2 is the largest untapped pool in the category (~$11-14k/mo). The tablet does not match Tier 2 search intent (nasal spray vs. homeopathic tablet), but the **Spray product does**. Today the Spray is running through the same shared campaign as the tablet and is unprofitable as a result.

What we'll do:
- Isolate the Spray into its own dedicated exact-match and phrase-match campaigns targeting nasal-specific queries (`dog nasal spray`, `dog nasal congestion`, `nasal drops for cats`).
- Strengthen Spray listing copy and imagery to match nasal-spray search intent.
- Test scaling carefully (spray AOV is lower; ROAS targets are different than the tablet).

**Expected impact:** $1,000-1,500/month in incremental Spray revenue at maturity. This is the single biggest unlock in the audit.

### Lever 3: Listing Improvements on the Tablets (60-90 days)

The tablet listing wins the funnel today, but two specific gaps would lift conversion further on the same traffic:
- **Bullets:** Currently 399 characters across 5 bullets. Best practice is ~200 characters per bullet. We will rewrite to address the three real purchase objections: "will this work for my breed," "how do I dose this," and "is homeopathic safe."
- **Main image:** Plain product box. We will introduce a warmer lifestyle cue (peaceful sleeping pet), a trust badge ("Since 1995" / "Made in USA"), and a clearer "20 Tablets" callout.

**Expected impact:** 5-10% lift in conversion rate on existing traffic. Compounds with Levers 1 and 2.

## 90-Day Plan

**Weeks 1-2: PPC Cleanup**
- Restructure broad-match campaign; launch dedicated exact-match campaigns for proven winners.
- Pause/negate wasted spend (human-intent terms, irrelevant pet-bed terms).
- Shift placement bids to Top of Search.
- Isolate Spray into a separate campaign.

**Weeks 3-6: Spray Activation + Listing Prep**
- Build Spray-specific Tier 2 campaigns; test bidding strategy.
- Rewrite tablet bullets, design new main image, prep refreshed gallery.
- Begin systematic review solicitation flow.

**Weeks 6-10: Publish + Scale**
- Publish new tablet listing assets.
- Scale Spray PPC based on weeks 3-6 learnings.
- Launch small branded defense campaign (2-3% of budget) to protect branded queries from competitor bidding.

**Weeks 10-12: Evaluate**
- Review tablet CVR lift from listing changes.
- Review Spray ROAS curve; decide whether to scale further or hold.
- Decide on category-expansion plays (content, vet partnerships) for the next quarter.
