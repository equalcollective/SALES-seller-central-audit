# ASIN Selection - Abokichi Inc. (Canada)

## Catalog Summary

Abokichi CA sells the same **OKAZU Japanese Chili Miso Oil** line as the US storefront, plus a newly-launched **ABO Matcha** product. The CA catalog is rolled up under a single master parent (B07L53JJVR) covering the Mild, Spicy, Curry, and Sansho children, with separate parents for the Variety Pack, the Sansho variant, and the Curry 2-pack.

Effectively there are **3 distinct chili miso oil products** (Spicy, Mild, Curry), sold in singles, 2-packs, and a 3-flavour variety bundle, plus one Sansho variant and one new matcha SKU.

## Key Observations from Step 1

**Insights:**
- **CA scale dwarfs US.** The CA storefront does ~$472K trailing-12-month vs the US storefront's ~$15K. The strategic frame is different: CA is an actively managed, profitable account that needs scaling and efficiency work; US is essentially a dormant account that needs a relaunch.
- **CA has a much broader peak season than US.** Revenue climbs Oct → Nov ($44K → $58K all-time peak), holds through Jan ($53K, $53K), then steps down to a stable $22-34K spring/summer band. November is the peak, not December. This is a 12-month curve, not a 1-month spike. To verify via SQP in Step 3.
- **Ad relaunch is recent and is working.** CA had **zero ad spend through Jan 2026** then launched in Feb 2026 ($1.8K spend → ROAS 3.47), scaled in Mar 2026 ($5.7K spend → ROAS 3.63), and held in Apr 2026 ($3.5K → ROAS 3.98). Healthy directionally - ROAS improving as the campaigns mature.
- **P0 (Spicy Single) is doing the work of the brand.** $22K/month at ~19% CVR with $3-4K of ad spend supporting it. P1 (Mild Single) was tracking at half of P0's monthly run rate until April.
- **P1 (Mild Single) collapsed in April.** Sales dropped from $8.3K (Mar) to $193 (Apr) - a 98% drop, alongside a buy box drop from 93% to 69% and a sessions drop from 3,220 to 148. This is more severe than the Apr-May 2025 P0 buy box dip in the US audit. Most likely a stockout or listing suppression event on this specific child.
- **The Spicy 2-pack (B088YKQV2K) buy box is in active collapse.** Went from 99.5% (Feb) → 61.5% (Mar) → 51.3% (Apr). Sales dropped from $5.4K → $1.9K → $1.2K. Since Abokichi is a private label, this looks like a MAP-related buy box suppression, same pattern as the US Mild parent in late Mar–Apr 2026.
- **Two SKUs are leaving organic-only revenue on the table.** Variety Pack (B07MCYB2VV) does $16.7K with $104 ad spend at 32x ROAS. Mild 2-pack (B088YNXZ4B) does $5.3K with $142 ad spend at 4.4x ROAS and 24% CVR. Both are credible scaling candidates.

**Things to Investigate:**
- **P1 (Mild Single, B07JWCDC4D) Apr collapse:** verify via Keepa in Step 2 whether this was a stockout (sales rank spike, sessions zero), a price-change event (buy box drop only), or listing suspension. The signature in seller analytics (buy box 93% → 69%, sessions 3,220 → 148) is more consistent with a stockout than a MAP event because sessions evaporated alongside buy box.
- **Spicy 2-pack (B088YKQV2K) buy box collapse:** confirm with Keepa pricing history that this is the same MAP/3P-seller pattern, not a stockout. If it is MAP, the price control fix is one MAP enforcement notice away.
- **November 2025 buy box dip (75.6%) at the all-time peak month:** unusual. Worth checking whether peak demand exposed an inventory constraint at the very moment ads should have been running (none were, since ads only launched Feb 2026).
- **ABO Matcha (B0F4BXF5FM) launch status:** new SKU with zero sales but listed. Confirm if this is pre-launch staging or live with no traffic.

**Questions for the Seller:**
- "P1 (Mild Single, B07JWCDC4D) went from $8.3K in March to $193 in April with sessions dropping from 3,220 to 148. Was this a stockout, a listing suspension, or something else? This is the single most important Q because it dictates whether P1 is a recovery play or a structural issue."
- "Buy box on the Spicy 2-pack (B088YKQV2K) has slid from 99.5% in Feb to 51.3% in Apr on what should be a private-label listing. Have there been recent price changes or have new 3P sellers latched onto the listing? MAP enforcement looks like the likely fix."
- "Ads launched in Feb 2026 after no spend at all in 2025. What was the trigger - new agency, internal capability, freed-up cash? We want to understand the budget envelope going forward."
- "Was the November 2025 peak ($58K all-time) constrained by inventory or buy box (which dipped to 75.6% that month)? Important for sizing the Q4 2026 plan."
- "Is ABO Matcha (B0F4BXF5FM) a live launch, a staged listing, or a long-term roadmap product? Determines whether it needs ad and listing attention now or later."
